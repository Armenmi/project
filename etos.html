ETOS school

____

The ETOS initiative is a program that includes continuous activities of students, teachers and parents of the school to promote universal human values: Ethics, Tolerance, Openness and Solidarity (ETOS) throughout the life and work of the school.

In 2019, our school, in cooperation with the TPO Foundation, became part of the ETOS initiative. Since then, we have been working intensively to promote this initiative and highlight its importance. Cooperation with ETOS has provided our students and professors with an excellent opportunity to hold various projects, creative workshops and ideas that help our students and professors develop human values, broaden horizons, see problems in the community and find solutions for them, poogna people and become better members of society

Earth Day, Anti-Violence Day, strengthening of intercultural competences, conflict resolution methods are just some of the projects and workshops in which our students and professors participated, as well as our guests from other schools. We can be sure that such projects have a great impact on students and help them pay attention to important things and become better people.

International Day of Peace

| Muhamed Gusic | 22. Septembar, 2022. |

Members of the "Critical Thinking Club" section from JU Third High School - Sarajevo took part in the peace event "NO WARS NO VIOLENCE" which took place in the premises of the Children's Home - Grbavica, on the occasion of the International Day of Peace, September 21, 2022. in the joint organization of the forum ZFD in BiH, the Peace Building Network Association and the Center for Peace Education UNSA - FF. The International Day of Peace was established by the General Assembly of the United Nations in 1981 for the purpose of marking and strengthening the ideal of peace among all peoples and countries, and on this occasion, today we present the peace activism of young people in local communities throughout Bosnia and Herzegovina...

"16 days of activism" in the Third Gymnasium-Student Council marks this important worldwide campaign

| Sumeja | 30. Novembar, 2022. |

"16 Days of Activism" has been celebrated in our school for several years now. It is a worldwide campaign that includes five important international dates that connect women, violence and human rights: November 25 - International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women December 1 - World AIDS Day December 3 - International Day of Persons with Disabilities December 6 - Anniversary of the Montreal Massacre December 10 - International Human Rights Day. On Tuesday, November 23, 2021, the student council and professor Sedžida Hadžić launched a campaign in our school with the following activities: watching a film about violence against women, holding two interactive workshops about violence...
