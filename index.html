About the school

Meet the school

____


In one of the most beautiful parts of Sarajevo, Wilson's Promenade, our Third Gymnasium was built, which began its educational work back in 1948. Despite changing its name throughout its history, it remains one of the best gymnasiums in Sarajevo.
We achieve continuous international cooperation through the exchange of students and teachers with schools of Sweden, Austria, France, Denmark, Switzerland, Germany and other countries.

WHAT TO STUDY?

Directions we offer

____

It's important to make a good decision and deal with what interests us. We can find something for everyone.

Mathematical and informatics direction

You are good logicians, you are good at mathematics and would like to see in what ways it is applied? This course is for you.

Natural direction

If you see yourself as a little researcher who is interested in natural phenomena and the world around us, natural direction is the right choice.

Information technology

If you are interested in digital devices, programming and would like to see what the IT sector offers, this direction is right for you.

General direction​

Still not sure what you're interested in, or maybe you're interested in a lot of things? The general direction is an excellent choice for acquiring knowledge in different fields.

Social direction​

If you are a social person and are interested in the history, culture and lifestyle of people from all over the world, in this direction you will find what you are interested in.

Language direction​

How many languages do you know, that's what you're worth! If you learn foreign languages easily and quickly and want to learn even more, this is more than an obvious choice.
