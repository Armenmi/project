European Day of Languages

| Muhamed Gusic | 27. Oktobar, 2022. |

Europäischer Tag der Sprachen This year, as in previous years, we celebrated the European Day of Languages in German language classes. The fourth grades of all elective areas and the third grades of the DSD program worked with their teacher Džaninanović Džemilamna on various materials dedicated to multilingualism and the importance of learning foreign languages, language policy in the EU, language and identity, and learned about general facts about language, language families, sign language language and engaged in many other educational and entertaining contents.
