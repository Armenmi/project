International Day of Peace

| Muhamed Gusic | 22. Septembar, 2022. |

Members of the "Critical Thinking Club" section from JU Treća Gymnasium - Sarajevo, took part in the peace event "NO WARS NO VIOLENCE" which took place in the premises of the Children's Home - Grbavica, on the occasion of the International Day of Peace, September 21, 2022. in the joint organization of the forum ZFD in BiH, the Peace Building Network Association and the Center for Peace Education UNSA - FF. The International Day of Peace was established by the General Assembly of the United Nations in 1981 for the purpose of marking and strengthening the ideal of peace among all peoples and countries, and on this occasion we present today the peace activism of young people in local communities throughout Bosnia and Herzegovina...

The victory of our basketball players

| Muhamed Gusic | 20. Septembar, 2022. |

The team of the Third High School took first place at the "Sketch basketball" tournament in the category up to IVth grade, which was held from Sunday (18.09) to Monday (19.09) in front of the BBI Center. In the final, they beat the Fourth High School with a score of 10 to 8. The team members are students Logan Mioč, Mak Selimović, Davud Japalak and Faris Aljić. Congratulations to our students.

The BiH delegation for the UNESCO Peace Center and the representative of Europe at their program

| Muhamed Gusic | 5. Septembar, 2022. |

Our student, Ena Hajdarević, spent her vacation on the east coast of the USA. Namely, after the time she spent first as a participant in many conferences, activities and programs of the UNESCO Peace Center, she had the opportunity to be part of the staff at their summer program. UNESCO Center for Peace IMUN – STEAM Summer Camp is a summer school with two mentioned directions that takes place as one of their activities, and the goal is to make youth leaders of peace. It consists of an IMUN program that offers participants workshops and the opportunity to learn more about diplomacy, public speaking and leadership.
